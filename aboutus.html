Project Overview

Welcome to our project implementation for a Restaurant Food Ordering System.

Our Purpose

Our system allows customers to order their foods via bring-your-own-device (BYOD). With the intention to help cut down on the operational cost and providing customers with a enhanced ordering experience!

Innovative Features

What sets us apart? Our system incorporates innovative features such as personalized menu recommendations and a random order generation for those who can't make up their mind on what to eat!

Hardware Infrastructure

To deliver a seamless experience, allowing customers to access our menu page on any web browser, we used AWS Cloud EC2 instance to host our webpage! This scalable and reliable setup ensures optimal performance and accessibility.
